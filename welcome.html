Welcome to laziest browser

Type a tiny prefix, press Enter, and let the router launch AI, browser, or crypto tools instantly.

Open Options

1. Add shortcuts

Head to Options, import a preset pack (AI, Browser, Web3), or add your own. Each mapping pairs a prefix with a URL that contains %s for the query.

2. Trigger from the address bar

Type your prefix plus search terms in Chrome’s address bar and press Enter. Example: ,vector databases sends the prompt to ChatGPT; //privacy opens Bing results.

3. Drag to prioritize

Drag the colored pill to rank your shortcuts. Imports respect your order and make your go‑to prefixes easier to scan.

Pro Tips

  • Shortcuts live in Chrome Sync storage, so they follow you across devices.
  • Prefix queries on Google results redirect automatically—no need to switch providers first.
  • Special phrase: start with 今天什么新闻 to jump to ChatGPT with your topic.
