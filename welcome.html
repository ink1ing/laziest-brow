Welcome to laziest browser

Type a tiny prefix, hit Enter, and jump straight to the tool you need.

Open Options

1. Add shortcuts

Open the Options page and import a preset pack (AI, Browser, Web3) or add mappings manually. Each shortcut maps a prefix to a target URL with %s as the query placeholder.

2. Trigger from the address bar

Focus Chrome's address bar, type your prefix followed by search terms, and press Enter. Example: ,vector databases jumps to ChatGPT, //privacy searches Bing.

3. Drag to prioritize

Reorder shortcuts in the Options list by dragging the colored pills. The order determines how presets import and how you scan your favorites.

Pro Tips

  • Shortcuts live in Chrome Sync storage, so they follow you across devices.
  • The extension also intercepts Google result pages; paste a prefix query into Google and it will redirect automatically.
  • Special phrase: start with 今天什么新闻 to jump to ChatGPT with your topic.
